# chargeforum-policy

Privacy Policy for CHARGEnorth Forum

Effective Date: Nov 29, 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We collect the following information when you use CHARGEnorth Forum:
- Email address (for account creation)
- Username (chosen by you)
- Posts, comments, and images you share
- Voting activity on posts

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use your information to:
- Provide the forum service
- Send notifications about replies to your posts
- Moderate content to keep the community safe
- Improve the app experience

3. DATA STORAGE
Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud).
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. DATA SHARING
We do NOT share your personal information with third parties.
Your posts are visible only to other CHARGEnorth members.

5. YOUR RIGHTS
You can:
- Delete your posts at any time
- Request account deletion by contacting us
- Export your data

6. COOKIES
We do not use cookies in the app.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
This app is intended for high school students (ages 13+).
We comply with COPPA regulations.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy and will notify users of changes.
